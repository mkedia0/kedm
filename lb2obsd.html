Letterboxd → Obsidian

A system that transforms your Letterboxd watch history into an Obsidian graph and structured database.

What this is

This project connects film logging data from Letterboxd to Obsidian, turning passive viewing history into a structured, queryable knowledge graph.

Why I built it

I wanted a way to move from passive consumption of films to active reflection and pattern recognition. Instead of just logging movies, the goal was to build a system that surfaces relationships between them.

How it works

  • Scrapes / exports Letterboxd watch history
  • Parses films into structured metadata (title, year, tags)
  • Converts entries into Obsidian-compatible markdown notes
  • Builds links between films to form a graph structure

What it enables

  • Visualizing film taste as a network
  • Finding patterns across genres / directors / moods
  • Turning media consumption into a knowledge system
